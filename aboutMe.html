About Me

Hi, I'm Pedro and I graduated from UCSD in December of 2020. I studied design and interaction in the Cognitive Science department, and have been teaching myself to code over the last year.

When I'm not sitting in front of my computer screen, you can probably find me on a soccer field. I started playing soccer when I was five and have been playing ever since. In college, I was a goalkeeper on the UCSD Men's Soccer team and was lucky enough to be the first choice for three years. Nowadays, I'm eagerly awaiting the return of men's leagues so I can start to relive my college glory days.

My other hobbies include surfing, playing the guitar, and exploring everything SoCal has to offer. My favorite places that I've visited so far have to be the Glamis sand dunes and the cliffs at La Jolla. I'm convinced that there isn't a prettier sight in the world than a sunset at Gliderport.

Thank you for reading a little bit about my life, I look forward to working with you!
